About the Nigerian Army

All affairs of the Nigerian Army are coordinated by the Nigerian Army Council (NAC) and training centers are supervised by the Training and Doctrine Command (TRADOC) headquartered in Minna. The army has 8 Divisions in all geopolitical zones of the country with different Areas of responsibilities which cover the entire country and a Training and Doctrine Command headquartered in Minna, Niger State.
